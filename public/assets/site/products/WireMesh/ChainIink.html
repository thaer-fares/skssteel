Chain Iink Fence

Chain link fence,also known by some as cyclone fence,is one of the most popular choices of fence for both light residential to heavy commercial fence applications. It can be used as garden fences,decorative fences,expressway ,railway ,barbed wire fences,chain link fences,expand metal fences etc. It is also suitable for houses and properties,companied,recreation areas,garden parks.All kinds of colors can be coated according to customers' requirements.

Material

low-carbon steel wire ,galvanized steel wire

Surface Finish:

galvanized,PVC coated or PE powder coated

Advantage:

1>:High strength
2>highly durable
3>good steel nature capacity,
4>wonderful shape,
5>wild field of vision,
6>easy to install,feel comfortable and bright.

Process:

low carbon iron wire ---making folds/curves----parkerising---electric galvanized/hot-dipped---PVC coatede /spraying---packing
